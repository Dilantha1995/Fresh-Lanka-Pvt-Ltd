Our story

About Fresh Lanka

We started Fresh Lanka with one idea: that the freshness of a Sri Lankan harvest should reach as many tables as possible — down the road, and around the world.

Who we are

Rooted in Sri Lanka, reaching the world

Fresh Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is a supermarket chain and global fresh-produce exporter, founded in 2026. Sri Lanka's soil and climate produce extraordinary vegetables, fruits, spices and betel leaves — and we built this company to share them properly.

At home, our stores give shoppers a fresher, friendlier grocery experience. Abroad, we connect importers and wholesalers with island-grown produce, handled with the care it deserves. Two halves of the same mission: get great food from the people who grow it to the people who enjoy it, with as little lost along the way as possible.

Our mission

To deliver honest, fresh, Sri Lankan food — affordably at home and reliably abroad — while giving local farmers a fair, direct route to market.

Our vision

To become a trusted name that carries the taste of Sri Lanka across the globe — known for freshness, fairness and quality you can count on.

Our values

Freshness without compromise, honesty in every order, respect for the farmers we work with, and care for the communities and land that feed us.

Leadership

Meet the co-founders

The people behind Fresh Lanka — leading with a shared belief in fresh food, fair trade and Sri Lankan quality.

Managing Director

S S D C Senadheera

As Managing Director, Mr. Senadheera sets the vision and direction for Fresh Lanka. He's driven by a simple belief — that Sri Lanka grows some of the world's finest produce, and that local farmers deserve a direct line to global markets. He leads the company's growth across both retail and export, and the partnerships that make it possible.

Director of Operations

Suresh Jeewan

As Director of Operations, Mr. Jeewan keeps the promise of freshness running on time. He oversees sourcing, grading, packing and logistics — the day-to-day engine that moves quality produce from farm to shelf, reliably and at scale, whether the destination is a local store or an overseas port.
