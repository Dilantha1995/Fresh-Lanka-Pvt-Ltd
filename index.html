Our signature

Premium Sri Lankan betel leaves

Betel — known at home as bulath — carries deep cultural meaning across South Asia and the Middle East, and demand for top-grade leaves keeps growing.

We work directly with betel growers, hand-pick at the right maturity, grade by leaf size and colour, and pack with care so every consignment arrives green, glossy and fresh.

Why Fresh Lanka

Freshness you can trace, quality you can trust
