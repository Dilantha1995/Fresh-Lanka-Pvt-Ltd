Quality & standards

Why we matter

Anyone can move boxes of produce. We protect freshness, build trust with growers, and make sure what reaches your shelf is worth selling — and worth coming back for.

Our standards

Six commitments behind every crate

Freshness is the result of dozens of small, deliberate decisions. These are the ones we never compromise on.

Direct from farmers

We buy straight from the growers we know and trust — fewer hands, fresher produce, and a fairer deal for the people who grow it.

Strict grading

Every item is sorted and inspected. Only produce that meets our size, ripeness and condition standards earns a place in your order.

Cold-chain logistics

Temperature-controlled handling and ventilated, protective packing keep produce in peak condition from harvest right through to arrival.

Food safety & hygiene

Clean handling at every stage — sorting, washing where needed, and careful packing — so quality and safety travel together.

Freshness, on time

We move quickly because freshness is time-sensitive. Tight scheduling means shorter journeys from field to your shelf.

Sustainable & local

Every order supports Sri Lankan farming communities and encourages responsible, long-term growing practices.
